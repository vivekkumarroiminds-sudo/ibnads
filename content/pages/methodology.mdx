## Our promise

Every review on ibnads is based on genuine hands-on testing. We buy or independently obtain the things we review, follow a documented protocol, and publish our reasoning — not just a score.

## How we score

We assign a primary rating from 0 to 5 stars. Where it adds clarity, we also publish **sub-scores** (for example: Sound, Comfort, Value) so you can weigh what matters to you. Sub-scores are averaged transparently, never to inflate a result.

| Stars | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| 4.5–5.0 | Exceptional — a category leader |
| 4.0–4.4 | Very good — recommended |
| 3.0–3.9 | Solid with caveats |
| Below 3.0 | We can't recommend it |

## Testing protocols

- **Products** — measured benchmarks (battery, performance, durability) plus real-world use over at least one week.
- **Software** — evaluated on real workloads, across every platform it supports, including offline and edge cases.
- **Media** — assessed by an editor with subject expertise, judged on craft and against its genre.

## Independence

Our editorial team decides scores. Brands never see reviews before publication and cannot pay for a better rating.

## Affiliate disclosure

ibnads is reader-supported. When you buy through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission at no extra cost to you. These commissions never influence our scores or which products we recommend. Affiliate links are marked and carry a `sponsored` attribute.
