## Why we exist

ibnads was built on a simple frustration: most "reviews" online are rewritten spec sheets. We test things ourselves, document how, and tell you the truth — including when something isn't worth your money.

## What we cover

We review **physical products**, **software and apps**, and **media** — books, movies, and games — with the same hands-on rigor across all three.

## Our standards

- We test before we publish.
- We disclose our affiliate relationships clearly. See our [methodology](/methodology#affiliate-disclosure).
- We correct mistakes and date every update.

## Get in touch

Have a product you'd like us to test, or a correction? We'd love to hear from you.
