## Competitor SERP analysis ("SmartyDrone review")

| Competitor type | Strengths | Weaknesses (our opening) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Syndicated PR pages (valleyrecord/kentreporter/rentonreporter "marketplace") | Domain authority of host newspaper; rank fast | Paid PR, uniformly positive, zero real testing, no honest cons, often outdated/duplicated |
| Affiliate "review" blogs (thegadgetsavvy, reviewpadho, techbullion, etc.) | Keyword-targeted, FAQ-stuffed | Thin, regurgitate marketing, many domains **already parked/dead**, no E-E-A-T |
| Scamadviser / trust-check | Credible legitimacy signal | Not a review; no buying guidance or alternatives |
| Funnel + clone sites (.co.uk, .company.site) | Control the narrative | Pure sales, no objectivity |

**Conclusion:** the SERP is dominated by thin, commission-driven, uniformly positive content — much of it ephemeral. **Nobody is serving the genuine "is this legit + what should I buy instead" intent with durable, expert, honest depth.** That gap is the entire ranking thesis.
