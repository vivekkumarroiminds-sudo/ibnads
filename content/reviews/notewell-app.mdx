## Overview

Notewell sets out to prove that a privacy-first note app doesn't have to feel slow or stripped-down. After migrating a 2,000-note vault into it, we mostly agree.

## Features

The editor covers the essentials — markdown, backlinks, tags, and quick capture — without the bloat. Search is instant.

## Privacy

Everything is end-to-end encrypted before it leaves your device, and the company can't read your notes. This is the headline feature and it's done right.

## Pricing & value

The free tier is usable but capped at 100 notes. Pro at $6/mo is fair, though slightly above some non-encrypted rivals.

## The verdict

The best private note app we've tested — with collaboration the one missing piece.
