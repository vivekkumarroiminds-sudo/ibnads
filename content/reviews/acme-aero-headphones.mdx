## Overview

The Acme Aero target the sweet spot of the headphone market: people who want serious active noise cancellation (ANC) and long battery life without paying flagship prices. After two weeks of daily commuting, calls, and listening tests, they earn a spot near the top of our [best noise-cancelling headphones](/roundups/best-noise-cancelling-headphones) roundup.

## Sound quality

Out of the box the tuning is warm with a gentle bass lift. Detail retrieval is strong for the price, and the soundstage is wider than we expected from a closed-back design.

## Noise cancellation

This is where the Aero punch above their weight. On a busy train the low-frequency rumble all but disappears.

## How we tested

We measured battery life under continuous playback at 50% volume with ANC on, ran latency tests over Bluetooth, and logged comfort over multi-hour sessions. See our full [methodology](/methodology).

## The verdict

A confident, well-rounded pair that does the important things — ANC, battery, and connectivity — better than anything else near $200.
