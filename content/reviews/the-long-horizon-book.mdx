## Overview

Mara Velez's debut spans three generations aboard a slow generation ship, but its real subject is much smaller: how people forgive one another.

## Characters

The cast is the book's triumph. Each viewpoint character is fully realized, flawed, and easy to ache for.

## Pacing

The middle act tests your patience before the final hundred pages snap everything into focus.

## The verdict

A confident, emotionally rich debut — recommended for fans of literary, character-driven science fiction.
